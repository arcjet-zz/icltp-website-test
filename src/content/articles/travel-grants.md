<div style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.6; color: #333333; text-align: left;">
<p style="margin: 0 0 24px 0;">The ICLTP Travel Grants are established to support a limited number of outstanding students who demonstrate excellence in plasma physics research. These grants are intended to offset travel costs for presenting their latest findings at ICLTP. The Grants can also cover parts of the conference registration and hotel room costs.</p>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Eligibility Criteria</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0;">To be eligible for a nomination, the candidate must meet the following criteria:</p>
<ul style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 32px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Must be a student in the field of low temperature plasma science.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Must have submitted an abstract as first author for either an Oral or Poster presentation. The abstract should be original (i.e., not published elsewhere).</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Research interests must align with the conference topics.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;">Priority will be given to applicants from institutions with limited travel funding.</li>
</ul>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Application Requirements</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0;">All nominations must be received by the abstract submission deadline: <strong style="color: #0148a0;">Dec 1st, 2026</strong>. The application should be uploaded via the official conference website. All nominations must be submitted in English as a single PDF document and must include:</p>
<ul style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 32px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Nomination Letter (Max 1 page):</strong> The nomination letter should address the following factors to be considered by the selection committee: 1) the advisor&rsquo;s assessment of the student, including research progress and the benefits to be gained by ICLTP attendance; 2) statement of financial need (i.e., would this grant enable attendance for additional students beyond student travel included in research grant budgets).</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Abstract:</strong> A copy of the submitted conference abstract.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Biographical Sketch (CV):</strong> Including a list of peer-reviewed publications, maximum two pages in total.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;"><strong>Statement of Nomination Reason:</strong> A brief description by the nominee on how attending ICLTP will impact their research on topics of ICLTP.</li>
</ul>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Review Process &amp; Obligations</div>
<ul style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 32px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">A Selection Committee will be established by the ICLTP Executive Committee to evaluate, select, and assess the nominations.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Please note that these grants are typically processed as reimbursements. Awardees must provide copies of boarding passes and flight receipts.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Awardees might be encouraged to do some volunteer work for a couple of hours during the conference.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;">A brief "Post-Conference Report" (approx. 200 words) summarizing their experiences at the conference must be submitted after the meeting.</li>
</ul>
<div style="background-color: #f0f5fa; border-left: 4px solid #0148a0; padding: 16px 20px; margin-bottom: 24px; border-radius: 0 4px 4px 0;"><strong style="color: #0148a0; font-size: 16px; display: block; margin-bottom: 10px;">Important Notes:</strong>
<ol style="margin: 0; padding-left: 18px; color: #333333;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 6px;">No more than one nomination may be submitted per research group.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 6px;">A nomination for a travel grant is separate from all other award nominations.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;">To attain the full benefit of the ICLTP conference experience, recipients of the travel grant are expected to attend the entire conference.</li>
</ol>
</div>
