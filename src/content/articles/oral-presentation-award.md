<div style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.6; color: #333333; text-align: left;">
<p style="margin: 0 0 24px 0;">The ICLTP Student Oral Presentation Award is established to recognize outstanding student oral presentations in the field of low-temperature plasmas. The award aims to encourage active student participation at ICLTP, promote excellence in original and high-quality research, and highlight effective scientific communication characterized by clarity, rigor, and professionalism. By enhancing the visibility and recognition of outstanding student contributors, the award also supports early-career development within the low-temperature plasma community.</p>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Eligibility</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 32px 0;">The nominee should be a student (undergraduate, master, or doctoral level) and is the first author and presenter of a talk (not a poster) at the ICLTP. The research should be relevant to the field of low-temperature plasmas.</p>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Nomination Requirements</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0;">The following documents are required for the nomination:</p>
<ol style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 32px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Abstract:</strong> A copy of the submitted abstract.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Personal Statement (1 page):</strong> A statement from the student outlining their role in the work, the motivation behind the study, the innovation of the work, and its relevance to low-temperature plasmas.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Curriculum Vitae (Max 2 pages):</strong> A CV of the student, including a list of publications.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;"><strong>Nomination Letter (1 page):</strong> A letter from the supervisor confirming the student's contribution to the work, the originality and innovation of the research, and the student's suitability for the award.</li>
</ol>
<div style="background-color: #f0f5fa; border-left: 4px solid #0148a0; padding: 16px 20px; margin-bottom: 24px; border-radius: 0 4px 4px 0;">The student presentation award winner will be announced at the banquet and honored with a <strong>certificate</strong> and a cash prize of <strong style="color: #0148a0; font-size: 18px;">$1,000 USD</strong>.</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 24px 0;">For questions contact the Chair of the ICLTP student oral presentation award committee, Dr. Li Wang (<a href="mailto:wang@aept.ruhr-uni-bochum.de" style="color: #0148a0; text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 1px solid #0148a0;">wang@aept.ruhr-uni-bochum.de</a>).</p>
