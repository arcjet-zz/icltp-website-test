<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Before you start</div>
<ul style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 30px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Know the number and the order of authors.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Proofread the abstract.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Abstracts are limited to 1 page.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px; text-align: justify;"><strong>Presentation Summary:</strong> Please provide a brief summary of your presentation written for a general audience. The summary should describe the "take-home" messages of your talk and their implications.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0;">If your abstract is accepted, you must be willing, present, and prepared to give the presentation.</li>
</ul>
<div style="font-size: 18px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; border-bottom: 2px solid #eaeaea; padding-bottom: 8px; margin-bottom: 16px;">Process &amp; Submission</div>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px 0; text-align: justify;">Contributed papers may be given orally in a 15-minute timeslot (12 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for questions) or as a poster. For either mode, authors must submit an abstract, which succinctly identifies the problem, describes the approach, and summarizes the status or result of the completed or intended intellectual contribution.</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 12px 0;"><strong>To submit an abstract, you must:</strong></p>
<ol style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 24px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Use the ICLTP sorting category list when submitting.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Know the correct ordering of authors and collaborators.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Submit abstract content.</li>

</ol>
