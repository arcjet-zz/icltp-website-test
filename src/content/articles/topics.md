<div style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.6; color: #000000;">
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">1. Fundamental Low Temperature Plasma Science</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Basic physical and chemical processes in low-temperature non-equilibrium plasmas: electron kinetics and energy distribution functions (EEDF), collision-radiative models, ionization waves, sheath dynamics, charged-particle transport, and elementary reaction rate coefficients in partially ionized gases.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">2. Plasma Diagnostics</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Experimental techniques for measuring plasma parameters: Langmuir probes, optical emission and absorption spectroscopy, laser-based methods (LIF, TALIF, Thomson scattering), mass spectrometry, microwave and THz diagnostics, fast imaging, and in-situ/non-invasive approaches with high spatial and temporal resolution.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">3. Plasma Modeling and Simulations</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Numerical modeling of low-temperature plasmas: global (0D), fluid, particle-in-cell/Monte Carlo (PIC/MCC), hybrid, and multi-scale models; coupling of plasma physics with surface processes, electromagnetics, chemistry, and radiation transport.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">4. Radio Frequency Plasmas</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Capacitively and inductively coupled RF discharges (CCP, ICP), very high frequency (VHF) and dual-frequency sources, electromagnetic effects, stochastic and secondary electron heating, plasma series resonance, wave propagation, and power coupling mechanisms.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">5. Atmospheric Pressure Microplasmas</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Microscale plasmas operating at atmospheric pressure: plasma jets, dielectric barrier microdischarges, microcavity and hollow-cathode discharges, array configurations, electron and metastable densities, stability, and applications enabled by high-pressure non-equilibrium conditions.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">6. Magnetized Plasmas</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Low-temperature plasmas under external magnetic fields: ECR, helicon, and magnetron sources; magnetized sheaths, E&times;B drifts, magnetic confinement effects, wave propagation and instabilities in magnetized RF and microwave plasmas.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">7. Plasma Processing Applications in Semiconductor Manufacturing and Advanced Materials</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Plasma etching, deposition, and surface modification for microelectronics: atomic layer etching (ALE), high-aspect-ratio and cryogenic etching, EUV photoresist processing, low-damage treatment of 2D materials (graphene, MoS₂, etc.), area-selective deposition, and advanced gate-stack engineering.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">8. Gas Conversion</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Plasma-assisted conversion of greenhouse and energy-carrier gases: CO₂ splitting and utilization, dry reforming of methane, ammonia synthesis, nitrogen fixation, hydrogen production, and energy efficiency optimization using DBD, gliding arc, microwave, and nanosecond-pulsed discharges.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">9. Plasma Catalysis</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Synergistic effects of non-thermal plasma and heterogeneous catalysis: in-plasma and post-plasma catalysis, catalyst activation by plasma-generated species, lowering of activation barriers, and applications in CO₂ hydrogenation, methane activation, VOC removal, and sustainable chemistry.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">10. Biomedical Applications</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Plasma medicine and biotechnology: direct and indirect treatment of cells and tissues, plasma-activated liquids, cancer therapy (plasma oncology), wound healing, sterilization, dentistry, blood coagulation, transdermal drug delivery, and selective targeting of diseased cells.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">11. Plasma-Liquid Interactions</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Fundamental processes at the plasma&ndash;liquid interface: generation, transport, and reactions of reactive oxygen and nitrogen species (RONS) in liquids, pH evolution, shock waves, cavitation, liquid-phase chemistry, and applications of plasma-activated water/media (PAW/PAM).</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">12. Propulsion</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Plasma-based electric and air-breathing propulsion systems: Hall thrusters, pulsed plasma thrusters (PPT), magnetoplasmadynamic thrusters, plasma-assisted combustion, low-power micro-propulsion for small satellites, and atmospheric-pressure plasma actuators for flow control.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 24px;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">13. Plasma Agriculture</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Applications of non-thermal plasma in agriculture and food processing: seed germination enhancement and pathogen inactivation via direct plasma or plasma-activated water, crop yield improvement, soil remediation, post-harvest treatment, pesticide degradation, and control of storage pests.</div>
</div>
<div style="margin-bottom: 0;">
<div style="font-size: 17px; font-weight: bold; color: #0148a0; margin-bottom: 6px;">14. DC, AC and Other Plasma Sources</div>
<div style="color: #000000; text-align: left;">Fundamental characteristics, operation regimes, and technological implementations of direct-current (DC) glow discharges, alternating-current (AC) and dielectric barrier discharges (DBD), nanosecond-pulsed, microwave, gliding arc, spark, corona, and arc plasmas; electrode configurations, breakdown mechanisms, transition between discharge modes, stability, and power-supply matching across a wide range of pressures and gases.</div>
</div>
</div>
