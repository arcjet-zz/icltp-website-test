<p style="margin: 0 0 16px 0;">China is a very safe destination for tourists and an excellent host country for conventions and conferences. Prospective attendees from most countries are required to obtain a visa to enter the People's Republic of China. The Organizing Committee advises participants to apply for their visas at least three months in advance.</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px 0;">To apply, prospective attendees must submit a passport valid for at least six months, a completed visa application form (available at your local Chinese Embassy or Consulate), and recent passport-sized photos to the Chinese Embassy or Consulate Visa Office. For detailed information, please consult the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China or your nearest Chinese Embassy.</p>

<ol style="padding-left: 20px; margin: 0 0 32px 0;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px;">To request an invitation letter, click on the request button below.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px; text-align: justify;">The name provided must exactly match the name as it appears on your passport. Any discrepancy between your passport and your invitation letter or other documentation could lead to a delay and/or denial of your visa application.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 12px; text-align: justify;">Please note that the ICLTP Organizing Committee is not authorized to assist with your visa application process beyond providing the official Invitation Letter. Should your application be denied, the organizing committee cannot alter the decision of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA), nor will we engage in any discussion or correspondence with MOFA or the Embassy on behalf of the applicant.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 0; text-align: justify;">As it can take approximately one month for the Embassy to process a visa application, please register as early as possible to ensure you have sufficient time to obtain your visa.</li>
</ol>
