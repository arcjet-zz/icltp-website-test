<div style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.6; color: #333333;">
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px 0; text-align: left;">The ICLTP will be held Monday - Friday, 19 &ndash; 23 April 2027 at the Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing, Beijing, China. ICLTP will gather leading scientists, engineers, and young researchers from around the world to discuss the latest advances in low temperature plasma science and technology. It aims to create a vibrant forum for the exchange of ideas, the presentation of frontier research, and the fostering of new collaborations. The conference will focus on the fundamental physics of plasma discharges, innovative diagnostic and modeling techniques, and emerging applications across energy, materials, environment, and medicine. By bridging disciplines and connecting generations of researchers, ICLTP seeks to inspire new perspectives and expand the horizons of plasma science.</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px 0; text-align: left;">It will be attended by international scientists at all career levels, including representatives of academia, industry, and national laboratories. Presentation formats include contributed, invited, and plenary talks, posters and exhibitions. In combination with coffee breaks, early career forum, and a banquet these events provide unique opportunities for technical discussions, networking, and recruitment.</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 24px 0; text-align: left;">ICLTP offers the following sponsorship and exhibitor options. If you are interested in sponsoring and/or exhibiting at this conference, contact Prof. Dr. Hai-Xing Wang (<a href="mailto:whx@buaa.edu.cn" style="color: #0148a0; text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 1px solid #0148a0;">whx@buaa.edu.cn</a>).</p>
