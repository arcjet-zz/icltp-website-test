Student awards

ICLTP Student Poster Awards

Recognition for outstanding student poster presentations.

Last updated13 Jun 2026
Prepared byLocal Organizing Committee

10% of the total poster award applications will be selected by the ICLTP Executive Committee and recognized at the banquet. The award consists of a certificate and a cash prize. The ICLTP gratefully acknowledges the important contributions students make to ICLTP.

Eligibility

To be eligible for the award:

  • The student must present a poster as first author.
  • Register for the poster judging before the application deadline.
Application Requirements

The student application should contain:

  • The student's abstract.
  • The name of the advisor.
Deadline for Nomination: February 1st, 2027
Contact Information

Committee Chair: Prof. He-ping Li (liheping@tsinghua.edu.cn)

General Inquiries: chenhanyu@imech.ac.cn
