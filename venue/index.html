Beijing guide

Venue

Venue details and transport options from Beijing airports.

Last updated13 Jun 2026
Prepared byLocal Organizing Committee
Transportation from Beijing Daxing International Airport (PKX)

Beijing Daxing International Airport (PKX) is located about 85 kilometers (53 miles) south of Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing. There are several convenient transportation options to reach the venue:

1. Airport Express + Subway
  • Duration: Approx. 1 hr 50 mins ~ 2 hrs 10 mins
  • Cost: Approx. 42 CNY (8 USD)
  • Route: Take the Daxing Airport Express to Caoqiao Station (approx. 19 mins). Transfer to Subway Line 10 and ride to Zhichun Road Station. Transfer to Subway Line 13 toward Dongzhimen and ride to Longze Station. From Longze Station, take Bus 315/870/889 or a short taxi ride to Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing.
  • Advantages: Economical and relatively predictable travel time; suitable for travelers familiar with Beijing’s subway system.
2. Taxi
  • Duration: Approx. 1 hr 10 mins ~ 1 hr 40 mins (depending on traffic)
  • Cost: 250 ~ 320 CNY (approx. 35 ~ 45 USD)
  • How to Use: Take an official taxi at the airport taxi stand and tell the driver "Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing".
  • Advantages: Door-to-door convenience; best for heavy luggage.
Transportation from Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK)

Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK) is located about 45 kilometers (28 miles) southeast of Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing. Several transportation methods are available:

1. Airport Express + Subway
  • Duration: Approx. 1 hr 30 mins ~ 1 hr 50 mins
  • Cost: Approx. 31 CNY (4.5 USD)
  • Route: Take the Capital Airport Express to Dongzhimen Station. Transfer to Subway Line 13 and ride to Xi'erqi Station. Finally, transfer to the Changping Line and ride to Shahe Station. From Shahe Station, take a short taxi ride to Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing.
  • Advantages: Affordable and commonly used by travelers.
2. Taxi
  • Duration: Approx. 50 ~ 80 mins
  • Cost: 150 ~ 200 CNY (approx. 21 ~ 28 USD)
  • How to Use: Follow signs to the taxi queue and tell the driver "Vision Plaza Shahe Bailing".
  • Advantages: Fast, direct, and convenient.
