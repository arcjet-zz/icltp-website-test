About the meeting

A forum for low-temperature plasma research.

ICLTP 2027 brings together researchers, engineers and students working from fundamental plasma physics to industrial, biomedical, energy and environmental applications.

The program is designed for technical exchange across disciplines, with plenary lectures, contributed presentations, poster sessions and dedicated support for student participation.

Scientific scope

Fourteen topics across the research continuum.

The scope connects fundamental processes and experimental methods with plasma sources, materials, energy, health and environmental applications.

01

Fundamentals & methods

  • Fundamental plasma science
  • Plasma diagnostics
  • Modelling and simulation
02

Sources & systems

  • Radio-frequency plasmas
  • Atmospheric microplasmas
  • Magnetized plasmas
  • DC, AC and other sources
03

Processes & applications

  • Semiconductor processing
  • Gas conversion and catalysis
  • Biomedical and liquid interactions
  • Propulsion and agriculture
